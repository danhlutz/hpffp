# quotRem and divMod

I suspect the following: 

* quotRem returns a tuple containing the quot and rem of a division

* divMod returns a tuple of the div and mod of a division

## now let's try it out

divMod (-13) 7 = (-2,1)

div (-13) 7 = -2
mod (-13) 7 =  1

quotRem (-13) 7 = (-1, -6)

quot (-13) 7 = -1
rem  (-13) 7 = -6
